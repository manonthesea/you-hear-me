Clack Clack Clack

1 Today’s Fergus lives under a low ceiling
2 in his indistinct spec home
3 in his under-engineered neighborhood
4 where property is a pervasive concept.

5 Clack clack clack clangs against pressboard walls.
6 Blue light permeates the windows to his mythical soul.
7 Opera like glass, unperceived and fragile, sings his purpose
8 to dust bunnies breeding in the corners of his life.

9 Clack clack clacking comes from his fingers.
10 His eyelids are curtains held up with duct tape.
11 Still-speaking slack jaw narrates his streaming dream aloud
12 to cluttered room buzzing with artificial power.

13 Upon his thrown as oil pressed through massive engine gears,
14 Fergus slumps into his spineless fate, 
15 heir to traded away authority  
16 for the right to buy.
